## Design Context

### Users
Everyday car owners who want actionable, transparent insights into their vehicle's health to avoid being overcharged. The environment requires a bilingual (English/Arabic) interface with LTR/RTL support for accurate localization of technical terms.

### Brand Personality
Bold, professional, and adult-like. The interface should naturally evoke feelings of confidence, cleanness, and unwavering trust.

### Aesthetic Direction
A pure dark mode-first approach showcasing a simple, clean, and highly professional layout. It must not look overly "AI-generated" or complex—restraint is key. Avoid the color purple unless absolutely necessary.

### Design Principles
1. **Uncomplicated Confidence**: Use robust, straightforward layouts to build trust and make diagnostic data easy to digest.
2. **Restrained Authenticity**: Maintain an adult-like professionalism. Avoid excessive AI visual tropes or embellishments that feel like they are "trying too hard".
3. **Contrast-Driven Clarity**: Utilize a highly readable dark theme as the primary experience, ensuring precise bilingual typography hierarchy without relying on purple hues.
